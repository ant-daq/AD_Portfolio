Simulating Natural Ventilation in Large Sports Buildings

Prediction of temperature and airflow patterns in the early design stages.

Approach

This research paper showcases the potential of a workflow capable of capturing the airflow and temperature distribution within large volume spaces such as indoor swimming pools. The workflow makes no use of CFD and thus accelerates the analysis with acceptable low error.

What I did

  • Ideation and research project management
  • Developed computational workflow to enable the balancing of an open-source natural ventilation analysis tool and a the EnergyPlus thermal analysis software
  • Analysed the results and compared against CFD analysis results
  • Generated Self-Organising-Maps to explore how the geometry, construction and opening properties affect the indoor thermal comfort and daylight performance
  • Published a conference paper in SimAUD 2016 and presented in London
